Key Terms

            
    
                
  • Front-line: The area at the front of the team most exposed to enemy attacks.
    • 
                
  • Off-Angle: An angle of attack that is not around where the core of your team is. Typically used by charaters good at giving off damage and running away. 
    • 
                
  • Core: Consists of supports and any damage charaters that are not taking an off angle.
    • 
                
  • Tank Charaters:Charaters that excel at soaking up damage; advancing and taking space agasint the enemy team.
    • 
                
  • Damgae Charater:Charaters that excel at eliminating enemies quickly.
    • 
                
  • Support Charaters:Charaters that excel at providing ultility and healing allies.
    • 
            

        


        
General Team Compositions

        

            

                
Poke

                

                    Poke compositions are typically used on maps with large open areas and typically consists of high burst damage charaters. This strategy relies upon eliminating enemies before fully engaging them. Getting a pick on an enemy typically allows your team to win a fight.
                

            

            

                
Brawl

                

                    Brawl compositions are typically used on maps with close close-quarters and consists of charaters with high survivability and sustained damage. This allows a team to win fights though attrition; outlasting the enemy team until your sustained damage overwhelms them.
                

            

            

                
Dive

                

                    Dive compositons can really be used on any type of map. This strategy relies on useing charaters with high mobility to outmaneuver the enemy team and get picks on key targets, such as support characters. The constant threat to the back lines allows your team to engage an enemy that is distracted with a backline threat. Further if key targets are eliminated your team should be able to aggressive push the enemy team.
                

            

        


        
Composition Comparison

        
        

            

                Marvel Rivals and Overwatch 2 are both hero shooter games, however dispite their similarites each of the previously mentioned team compositions play out a little differently.
            

        


        

            
Overwatch

            

                
Poke

                

                    In Overwatch, poke compositions rely heavily on ranged characters with high burst damage and precision. These compositions are effective on maps with long sightlines and open areas. They focus on dealing significant damage from a distance to weaken or eliminate enemies before engagements.
                

                
    
                    
  • Strengths: High burst damage, effective at controlling engagements from a distance.
    • 
                    
  • Weaknesses: Vulnerable to flanking and highly mobile enemies.
    • 
                    
  • Key Features: Long-range attacks, precision abilities, and map control.
    • 
                


                
Brawl

                

                    Brawl compositions in Overwatch focus on close-range engagements and sustained damage. These compositions thrive in maps with tight corridors and enclosed spaces. They rely on durability and consistent damage output to outlast opponents in prolonged fights.
                

                
    
                    
  • Strengths: High survivability, excels in close-quarters combat.
    • 
                    
  • Weaknesses: Struggles against compositions that can maintain distance and kite effectively.
    • 
                    
  • Key Features: Close-range abilities, high durability, and crowd control.
    • 
                


                
Dive

                

                    Dive compositions in Overwatch rely on mobility and coordination to eliminate key targets, such as enemy supports or damage dealers. These compositions are effective on maps with verticality and open spaces, allowing for quick engagements and disengagements.
                

                
    
                    
  • Strengths: High mobility, effective at disrupting enemy backlines.
    • 
                    
  • Weaknesses: Requires strong coordination and struggles against sustained damage compositions.
    • 
                    
  • Key Features: Mobility, burst damage, and target prioritization.
    • 
                

            


            
Marvel Rivals

            

                
Poke

                

                    In Marvel Rivals, poke compositions focus on ranged characters with abilities that deal consistent damage over time. These compositions excel at controlling the battlefield and forcing enemies to reposition. They are effective in maintaining pressure from a distance.
                

                
    
                    
  • Strengths: Battlefield control, strong at wearing down enemies over time.
    • 
                    
  • Weaknesses: Vulnerable to aggressive melee characters and fast-paced strategies.
    • 
                    
  • Key Features: Ranged attacks, utility abilities, and sustained pressure.
    • 
                


                
Brawl

                

                    Brawl compositions in Marvel Rivals emphasize durability and sustained damage. These compositions are effective in holding objectives and engaging in prolonged fights. They rely on characters with high survivability and crowd control abilities to dominate close-range encounters.
                

                
    
                    
  • Strengths: High durability, excels in holding objectives and prolonged engagements.
    • 
                    
  • Weaknesses: Struggles against compositions that can kite and maintain distance.
    • 
                    
  • Key Features: Close-range combat, crowd control, and area denial.
    • 
                


                
Dive

                

                    Dive compositions in Marvel Rivals rely on characters with high mobility and burst damage to eliminate key targets. These compositions are effective at disrupting enemy formations and creating chaos, allowing for quick eliminations and follow-up engagements.
                

                
    
                    
  • Strengths: High mobility, effective at isolating and eliminating key targets.
    • 
                    
  • Weaknesses: Requires precise execution and struggles against well-coordinated teams.
    • 
                    
  • Key Features: Agility, burst damage, and disruption tactics.
    • 
                

            

        

        

            

                
Summary of Differences

                

                    While both Overwatch and Marvel Rivals share similarities as hero shooter games, their team compositions and strategies differ significantly due to the unique mechanics and design of each game:
                

                
    
                    
  • Overwatch: Focuses on precision, burst damage, and map control. Compositions like Poke excel at long-range engagements, while Brawl thrives in close-quarters combat, and Dive emphasizes mobility and coordination to disrupt enemy backlines.
    • 
                    
  • Marvel Rivals: Emphasizes sustained pressure, battlefield control, and agility. Poke compositions focus on consistent damage over time, Brawl excels at holding objectives with durability, and Dive relies on high mobility and burst damage to create chaos.
    • 
                

                

                    These differences highlight how each game tailors its strategies to its unique gameplay mechanics, offering distinct experiences for players.
